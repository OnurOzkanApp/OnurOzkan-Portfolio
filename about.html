ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Onur, a software developer and AI enthusiast from Istanbul, Türkiye. I hold an Honours Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Toronto, where I specialized in Machine Learning and Data Mining. After living and working in Toronto, Ontario, Canada for 7.5 years, I have recently returned home and am now based in Istanbul.

Over the course of my career, I have contributed to a range of impactful projects that strengthened both my technical and collaborative abilities. As a Co-op Junior Software Developer with the Government of Ontario’s Children, Youth, and Social Services I&IT Cluster, I gained hands-on experience in software development and automation. More recently, as a Data Analyst at TELUS International AI Data Solutions, I focused on improving AI data accuracy, further sharpening my analytical and problem-solving skills. Today, I continue building projects like Timeline Raider (Unity, C#) and Mani! (2D Match-3). I love combining creativity with technology — from ML and data analysis to game development and interactive design. See more on the Projects page.
